What do we do?

We specialize in buying and managing household waste around all the rural areas of Manipur to create a cleaner and more sustainable environment.

By partnering with us, you contribute to reducing landfill waste and promoting recycling efforts, making a positive impact on the planet. Join us in our mission to turn everyday waste into opportunities for a greener future.

How to Sell Your Collected Plastics and Metals?

  • We appreciate your efforts in contributing to a cleaner environment by collecting plastics and metals. Follow these simple steps to sell your collected materials:
  • 1. Click on the "Contact us" button and navigate to the contact page.
  • 2. In the contact page section choose your preference and contact us to sell.
